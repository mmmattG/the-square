Version: 0.1.3
Date: 2026-05-27
  Features:
    - Added Space Age support for planets, Vulcanus, Fulgora, Gleba and Aquilo.
    - Added a per-player "Get cliff explosives" mod setting button to help recover from cliff generation blocking progression on Vulcanus.
  Changes:
    - This is a major update, with entire systems reworked to better fit Space Age.
    - Reworked the ingress/egress system around craftable anchors.
    - Removed the expansion point purchasing system. Ingress and egress anchors are now crafted and placed from inventory.
    - Anchor slots can now be clicked to configure ingress and egress directly, consuming the matching anchor item from the player's inventory.
