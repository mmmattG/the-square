# Grill-Me Conversation: Ingress Lore And Company Framing

Date: 2026-03-30

## Outcome

This conversation established the current lore direction for ingress, quota materials, and company messaging.

## Core Decisions

- Ingress comes from the company, not from local mining fiction and not from interplanetary shipping.
- The square is a controlled company facility where the engineer is confined and expected to produce quota materials.
- The company is cruel, delusionally upbeat, and never acknowledges the ethical problem.
- Science packs are treated as valuable opaque quota products rather than abstract research.
- Major science-band completions should be used for lore beats and expansion authorization messaging.
- Additional space is framed as privilege earned through useful output.

## Ingress Lore

- Ingress is company provisioning.
- Raw resources are delivered through approved ingress points.
- The ingress source should remain a compact point source.
- Long visible delivery lines were rejected because they weaken the fiction of easy anchor relocation.

## Ingress Texture Direction

- The ingress anchor should read as a closed circular hatch or manhole-like floor aperture.
- The hatch should be closed and inert when no receiving belt is nearby.
- Alt mode should still show what resource the ingress is assigned to.
- When active, items should rise via magnetic or antigravity levitation.
- The company should feel technologically far beyond the engineer.
- The visual target is clinical but worn from endless corporate reuse.

## Company Voice

- The opening should be explicit, but very brief.
- The tone should resemble corporate orientation material.
- The company should act as if everything is normal.
- It should be upbeat in ways that do not make sense given the engineer's situation.
- It should occasionally refer to the player's "accommodations" as a grotesque corporate joke.
- The normal official term for the square is `facility`.

## Expansion And Quota

- Expansion science is company intake, not abstract technological progress.
- Science bottles are valuable physical products the company wants.
- Different science packs correspond to different quota product classes.
- The purpose of those products remains opaque, but obviously important to the company.
- Major quota thresholds should unlock larger facility space.

## Follow-Up Work Identified

- Add in-game opening lore and milestone announcements for major science-band transitions.
- Explore ingress texture concepts that fit the hatch-plus-antigrav direction.
